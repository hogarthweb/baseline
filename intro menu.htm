Introductory Materials:

 

Hogarth Online: From Concept to Reality: includes discussion of how this site was developed as well as how and why specific primary and secondary materials were selected.

 

Hogarth, the Engraver: examines why Hogarth used engraving to distribute his message and includes information on print prices, methods and audiences.

 

How to Read Hogarth: gives the viewer advice for interpreting Hogarth's complex array of messages.
