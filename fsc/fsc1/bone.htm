The Four Stages of Cruelty: The First Stage of Cruelty, Detail.

 

A boy torments a dog by tying a bone to its tail.

 

Other commentary:

Shesgreen: a youth ties a bone to the tail of a pup that licks his hand gratefully in response (77).

Trusler: A boy tying a bone to the tail of his dog, while the kind-hearted animal licks his hand, must have a most diabolical disposition (134).
