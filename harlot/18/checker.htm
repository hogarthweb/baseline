A Harlot’s Progress, Plate 1, Detail.

 

The checker square indicates that Charteris and Gourlay are emerging from a tavern. Commentators have located the picture at the Bell Inn on Wood Street. Including the tavern sign emphasizes the vice present in the print.

Other commentary:

Shesgreen: Before the Bell Inn in Wood Street, Mary or Moll Hackabout, newly arrived in London, is caught between the aggressive agents of corrupt, who are set against the crumbling tavern wall, and the ordinary (and passive) middle-class people arranged around the solidly built home (18).
