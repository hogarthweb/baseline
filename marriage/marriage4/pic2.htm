Marriage à la Mode, Plate 4, Detail.

 

Jupiter and Io hint at the problematic seduction occurring below.

 

Other commentary:

Paulson, Hogarth’s Graphic Works: Jupiter embracing Io (which had unhappy consequences for Io) (273).

Lichtenberg: Next to this picture hangs the 'Consequences of Masquerades', in the story of the beautiful princess lo, where she, too, is bitten by the enraged Jupiter clad in his usual Thundercloud-domino. This is a copy of a very well-known presentation of that biting by Michelangelo Buonarroti, bought of course by her Ladyship for the original itself, and perhaps even paid for I as such (127).
