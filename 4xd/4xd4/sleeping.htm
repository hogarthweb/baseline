The Four Times of the Day: Night, Detail.

 

A linkboy’s flame threatens the sleeping beggars.

 

Other commentary:

Shesgreen: Beneath the ledge on which they stand, three homeless people huddle together in sleep, and a linkboy or guide blows his torch to a flame (45).

Paulson, Hogarth’s Graphic Works: Under the bulk a man and a woman are asleep in their "beggar's bagnio" (181).
