Beer Street, Detail.

 

Prosperity is indicated by the ongoing construction.

 

Other commentary:

Shesgreen: Four men work on its roof; three drink and wave their hats joyfully, the fourth descends to share some ale with the three tailors in the building across from them (75).

Trusler: on a house-top are four bricklayers, equally joyous (129).

Paulson, Hogarth’s Graphic Works: The workmen on the housetop are waving their caps in celebration of the day and of the beer they are drinking. The building is an ale house ("the Sun," with the checkerboard by the door) (208).
